Lyno simply Smart Defi



LYNO is a charity SmartDefi utility token on FEGex, operating on BSC network. Our mission is to provide vulnerable children from Africa and by extension all over the world with the much needed support to explore and discover their innate abilities that have been left to rot over the years.

Our Concern As Charity



We are especially concerned about the plight of kids in developing countries and the implication of having so many out of school children who due to various circumstances are left to roam the street constituting a nuisance to themselves and the larger society.

Our Mission as Lyno


Our charity mission is geared towards modeling responsible kids that would best fit the description LEADERS OF TOMORROW. The importance of education in the life of a child cannot be overemphasized, every child has the potential to be a star whose success would echo all over the planet, just with the right touch. We are LYNO (coined from Lyra and Nova, which when paired together aptly describe over objective: constellation of explosive stars). Our charity mission would center around Education, Hygiene, health and talent building and we want to ensure that each child enjoys the perfect blend for a secure and a future assured.

Our Platform



Ours is a platform where investors, donors, supporters and the general LYNO community can invest with the firm assurance that their funds is being strictly channeled for the intended purpose, with accountability, transparency and integrity being our watchwords.
